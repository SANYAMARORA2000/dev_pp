COVID-19 HEALTH AND SAFETY

We are taking extended measures to ensure the safety and wellness of our staff, customers, commodities and prepared foods. Operating under social distancing guidelines and following enhanced cleaning, hand-washing, sanitisation and hygiene protocols across all stores and facilities. We are taking reasonable measures to help people work from home. We are distributing face masks and gloves to our team members across our stores at the beginning of each shift. We have also implemented temperature screening checks for our team members and customers in all our stores and facilities.
